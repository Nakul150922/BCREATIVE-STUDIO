ABOUT THE STUDIO

We build films
for the thumb.

BCREATIVE STUDIO was founded on a simple idea: the most intimate screen in the world deserves the most ambitious storytelling.

01 — ORIGIN

Our story.

Founded in 2024, BCREATIVE began with an idea that had been in development for years: could the language of cinema translate to a 9:16 frame? Already dozens of episodes produced, the answer is yes — and audiences are watching.

We work with directors, writers and platforms to develop original IP, branded series, and documentary content engineered for vertical playback. Every shot is composed for a phone. Every cut serves attention.

02 — VALUES

What we stand on.

01

Cinema First

Format follows story. We treat every vertical frame with the same intention as a feature.

02

Audience Native

We shoot, edit, and pace for the way people actually watch — vertical, distracted, scrolling.

03

New Voices

04
